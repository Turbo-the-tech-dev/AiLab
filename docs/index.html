AiLab

Your laboratory for AI and Large Language Model (LLM) experimentation.

About the Project

AiLab is a new project dedicated to exploring the capabilities of AI and LLMs. It serves as a personal lab for conducting experiments, training models, and developing AI-powered applications. The project is currently in its initial scoping phase.

Current Features

As a new project, AiLab is currently a blank slate. The immediate focus is on defining the core features, which may include:

  • Model training and fine-tuning pipelines.
  • Evaluation scripts for assessing model performance.
  • Data processing and augmentation tools.
  • APIs for serving experimental models.

The Future of AiLab

The future of AiLab is open-ended and will be driven by experimentation and discovery. Potential future directions include:

  • Exploring novel LLM architectures.
  • Developing specialized models for specific domains.
  • Building creative and practical applications powered by the models developed in this lab.
  • Contributing to open-source AI research and development.

The project's progress and direction will be tracked through the issues in this repository.
